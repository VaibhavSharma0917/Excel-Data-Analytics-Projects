


# 📊 Excel Data Analytics Projects

> **A collection of end-to-end Excel business analytics projects demonstrating executive dashboards, financial reporting, sales analysis, customer performance analysis, and market intelligence through real-world business reporting scenarios.**

---

# 📌 Project Overview

This repository contains a collection of **business analytics reports** developed using **Microsoft Excel** and presented as professional PDF reports.

The projects simulate **real-world corporate reporting scenarios** for a global consumer electronics company, demonstrating how Business Analysts and Data Analysts transform raw business data into executive-level reports that support strategic decision-making.

The reports focus on customer performance, market performance, financial analysis, profitability tracking, and KPI reporting using industry-standard Excel techniques.

---

# 🎯 Business Objectives

- Analyze customer sales performance across multiple years
- Evaluate market performance against business targets
- Monitor company profitability using Profit & Loss statements
- Compare financial performance across fiscal years and global markets
- Track monthly business performance trends
- Build executive reports for data-driven decision-making

# 📊 Reports Included

## 📈 1. Customer Performance Report

### Objective

Analyze customer-wise sales performance across multiple fiscal years to identify revenue growth, high-value customers, and business opportunities.

### Report Highlights

- Customer-wise Net Sales Analysis
- Multi-Year Sales Comparison
- Year-over-Year Growth Analysis
- Customer Revenue Performance
- Executive Business Reporting

### Business Value

Supports sales teams in identifying top-performing customers, monitoring growth trends, and strengthening customer relationship strategies.

---

## 🌍 2. Market Performance vs Target Report

### Objective

Evaluate actual sales performance against predefined business targets across international markets.

### Report Highlights

- Country-wise Sales Analysis
- Target vs Actual Comparison
- Market Performance Evaluation
- Variance Analysis
- Target Achievement %

### Business Value

Helps management identify underperforming markets, monitor business performance, and optimize regional sales strategies.

---

## 💰 3. Financial Reports

The **Financial Reports** folder contains three executive-level Profit & Loss reports that provide a comprehensive financial overview of business performance.

### 📅 P&L Statement by Fiscal Year

Provides annual financial performance including:

- Net Sales
- Cost of Goods Sold (COGS)
- Gross Margin
- Gross Margin %

---

### 🌍 P&L Statement by Markets

Analyzes financial performance across global markets using:

- Net Sales
- COGS
- Gross Margin
- Gross Margin Percentage

---

### 📆 P&L Statement by Months

Tracks monthly business performance to monitor:

- Revenue Trends
- Monthly Gross Margin
- Seasonal Business Performance
- Profitability Trends

### Business Value

Enables management to monitor financial health, evaluate profitability, and support strategic planning through detailed financial reporting.
